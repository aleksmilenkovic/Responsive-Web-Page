Katarina and Aleksandar, in search of their own expression, play a top repertoire on the track of evergreen, jazz, pop rock, sharing only good values. Under the name Kate No More, they have been performing for two years and during that time they have been hired at places such as Wurst Platz Bar, Casino by Community, Betonski Brod, Republic Pub, Pub Čikoš (Pozarevac), Café Club Smederevo and many others. They also played at several restaurants such as the garden of Velika Skadarlija, Korcagin, Giardina and Zemun Rijeka. They also perform at private events and various celebrations. For the past 10 years, as a duo under a different name, but also in bands with colleagues, they have performed in famous city clubs (Fest, Optimist, Tram) and many others. As a guitarist for the international band Worldly Savages, Alexander has played at the most famous clubs and major festivals across Europe (Eurosonic, The Netherlands, PolkaBeats Germany, Mighty Sounds, Czech Republic, Zwarte Cross, The Netherlands, Les Musicaves, France, Revolution, Romania, Nishville, Serbia and many others ). Kate No More has a colorful repertoire ranging from jazz and soul standards and classic rock and roll to homemade pop rock and popular hits, delivering every show with a lot of love and energy. Whether it's a matinee (restaurant) type of gig or a real party, Kate No More is up to the task. The Kate No More Duo is also available in band variants.
